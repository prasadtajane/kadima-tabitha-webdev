About Me

My name is Tabitha Kadima. I grew up in Andover, Massachusetts but I'm originally from the Democratic Republic of Congo. I am currently a senior at Northeastern University majoring in Computer Science. I expect to graduate May of 2017. I have completed 3 co-ops, working mainly in application development for 6-7 months at Novartis Institute for Biomedical Research, PayPal and Boston Children's Hospital. I came to discover that mobile and web application development, is my passion. I'm hoping I can jumpstart my career at a company where I can be a great asset and that will help me grow and learn in my field. In my free-time, I enjoy creative writing, reading the plays of Henrik Ibsen and watching a classic black and white movie. I also study French and hope to one day become totally fluent. When the weather is nice I will occasionally go a hiking trail or go rock-climbing with friends. Feel free to contact me for more information!
