About Me

My name is Tabitha Kadima. I am a senior at Northeastern University studying computer science. After having multiple co-ops, I realized that my passion is in Web Applications Development. I hope to continue to pursue that after my graduation. My co-ops at Novartis, Paypal and Boston Children's Hospital and my academic courses allowed me to solidify my knowledge of the field. I have extensive experience implementing web applications using the MVC software architectural pattern of design.

I've also had experience working in the Agile work environment. I prefer a work but I can also be thrive working independently. I hoping to work for a company in the Boston area where I can be a valuable asset and that will help me grow in my career. I'm also willing to venture outside of the Boston area in cities such as Seattle, Portland, Denver, or even London.

When I find a time of solitude, I enjoy writing both poetry and prose. Occasionally, when the weather is nice, I'll take a hike or nature walk along a trail. If I not doing any of those things, I'm likely to be at the gym.
